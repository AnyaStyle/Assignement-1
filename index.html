ABOUT US

Our story began in 2015...

Even then, we realized that something flashed between us then, but we were afraid to admit it to ourselves since we had families. I remembered about you throughout all these years, I wanted to see you, but I could not afford to call you. Well, what could I tell you?

January 13, 2021 - it was a beautiful sunny day, I was sitting and boring in my office, and then I saw you walking along the pier... You have no idea how I jumped with happiness... my colleague was surprised, thinking that I saw a very close one me a person (yes! inside of me you were very close). You were confused when I ran up; we hugged, but I didn’t feel anything on your part... I still took the first step and TXTed you that I was glad to see you, but there was no continuation of our communication...

I continued to remember you sometimes.... What attracted me to you? Mysteriousness, self-confidence,ease of communication, charming smile and insanely kind eyes... But most importantly, your vibes are ideal for me (I did not know all your other virtues).

Saturday, July 10, 2021, 9 pm - I receive a text message from you. On the one hand, I was glad to receive it; on the other hand, I thought you had apparently gotten drunk and were looking for a company. If it were a day, I would have snapped and come to you, but then I simply replied that I was busy.

July 14, 2021 - another TXT from you and an offer to go to Ohakune... Unexpectedly, strange, but wildly attractive... It was interesting for me to spend time with you, not the trip itself.

July 23, 2021 - the trip that turned my mind upside down. I told myself - this is just fun, nothing serious can be... But I wanted to feel you more and more...

August 17, 2021 – lockdown happened… and suddenly we spent 24 hours a day for five weeks together. It was a magical time for me, even though we were home.

September 2, 2021 - you invite us to be a couple. I’m scared, but what if it won’t work out, but at the same time I understand that I am falling in love with you already... But you convinced me that everything would be fine.

Everything was okay until the end of 2021... I can’t say that it was great since I began to dissolve in you, to relax and let everything go by itself. And this cannot be done.

The beginning of 2022 is a mistake on a mistake... a lot of pretensions on my part... the accumulation of grievances on my part and, as a result, breakdowns... I understand that I was essentially wrong, and I am ashamed of it... This is a relationship, and it’s okay to swear a little, the main thing is to find compromises in the end.

You are the best, Joshua, and want to work on our relationship.

Forever yours,

Anya
